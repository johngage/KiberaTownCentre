TEGLA LOROUPE PEACE FOUNDATION
Tegla Loroupe Peace Foundation (TLPF) is a registered Charity in Kenya under the PERPETUAL SUCCESSION ACT, CHAPTER 164, LAWS of KENYA. The charity is in honour of Tegla Loroupe, a renowned international athlete who has vowed to use her track and field achievements, skills and friends to promote peaceful coexistence and socio-economic development of poor and marginalized pastoralists and agro-pastoralists men, women and children in the Greater Horn and Great Lakes Region of Africa.

The main purpose of the Foundation is to support and further initiatives that promote conflict resolution, peace building and poverty reduction initiatives that enhance livelihoods and resilience of poor people affected by and vulnerable to conflicts and civil strife in the world.
The Charity envisions a peaceful, prosperous and just world in which sports is a unifying and livelihood factor.

The Foundation runs four programmes namely: -
   Peace Building and Conflict Resolution Programme.
  Poverty Reduction Strategies.
  Gender Empowerment & HIV AIDS Mitigation
  Sports Promotion

The Foundation is composed of the following Board of Trustees who formulates policies to be implemented by the Management team under the Board’s monitoring and evaluation. Click to view BOARD OF TRUSTEES

   
TLPF'S VISION.
"A Peaceful, Prosperous and just world in which sports is a unifying and livelihood factor".
   
TLPF MISSION
"To improve Peace Building, Livelihoods and Resilience of poor people affected by and vulnerable to conflicts and civil strife in the world".
   
TLPF'S AMBITION.
"TLPF aims to become the leading international authority in the realm of conflict resolution and poverty reduction strategies. TLPF will build on its own distinctive and unique approach to conflict resolution and peace building while envisaging a lasting peace not only in the Sub Saharan Africa but also in other parts of the world wrecked and mutilated by conflicts".
